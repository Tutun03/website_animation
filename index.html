ABOUT DEPARTMENT
The Electrical Engineering Department at the Institute of Engineering & Management is committed to producing highly skilled engineers equipped with knowledge in IoT, Artificial Intelligence, and coding to meet the demands of Industry 4.0. Our pedagogy emphasizes practical work, research, and project-based learning to nurture competent and innovative engineers. Our faculty members, comprising academically proficient and industry-experienced individuals, employ analytical and practical teaching methods to instill conceptual understanding and foster teamwork among students. Specialized faculties cover various domains of Electrical Engineering, focusing on frontier technologies such as power quality studies, microprocessor applications, smart grid design, and non-conventional energy sources. We prioritize excellence in academics and encourage students to pursue knowledge for innovation, preparing them to excel in the modern technological landscape.

PO, PSO & PEO

PO, PSO & PEO

      Program Outcomes (POs):

1. Apply engineering knowledge effectively to solve complex problems.
2. Analyze and formulate solutions based on research and first principles.
3. Design solutions considering health, safety, and environmental factors.
4. Conduct investigations using research methods and data analysis.
5. Utilize modern engineering tools appropriately.
6. Assess societal and ethical implications of engineering solutions.
7. Understand and promote sustainable development practices.
8. Adhere to ethical principles and professional responsibilities.
9. Work effectively in teams and communicate engineering concepts clearly.
10. Apply engineering and management principles for project success.
11. Recognize the importance of lifelong learning and adapt to advancements.

       Program Specific Outcomes (PSOs):

1. Demonstrate comprehensive technical knowledge and laboratory skills.
2. Develop multidisciplinary capabilities and computational proficiency.
3. Cultivate professional skills, teamwork, leadership, and management knowledge.

       Program Educational Objectives (PEOs):

1. Establish successful careers in engineering and provide innovative solutions.
2. Pursue higher education and engage in research effectively.
